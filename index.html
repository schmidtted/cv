Who I am

I was born in the USA but have made New Zealand my home. I am a dual citizen and have lived and worked in New Zealand since 2004. I hold a Bachelor of Science in Computer Graphics, a Masters Certificate in Information and Telecommunication Systems, and a Masters of Business Administration.

I started my professional life as a US Naval Officer and served on several surface ships and have even spent a number of months on a submarine. Following my service in the Navy, I embarked on a career in IT with a focus on web application development; initially as a developer and progressing through to roles with aspects of account management, product management, business analysis, project management, leadership, and general management.

What gets me up in the morning is a company with solid values, a clear mission with a noble purpose, and a coherent strategy. I believe a great company is one that sees the unique talents of its employees and leverages them in a way that makes people feel fulfilled and able to do their best work.

No matter what company, position, project, or task I am involved with I always approach the situation with a curious mind and enjoy working on finding solutions to problems with others. Relationships are at the heart of everything I do and the lens through which I view the world.
